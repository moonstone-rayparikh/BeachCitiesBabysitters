About Us

What we're here for

Beach cities babysitting is a student run organization that aims to connect students looking to babysit with parents in need. As an organization, we try our absolute best to accommodate parents’ needs, so that we can provide a service that is often looked over, but very much needed. To extend our service for children and families, 15% of all babysitters' profits go to a charity that provides education for underprivileged & homeless kids!

The overall focus of our program is to make childcare more accessible by providing easy access to supportive, trustworthy, and reliable caregivers that are ready to give your child any support needed.

Our Pricing

We charge an hourly rate of $20, with an additional $5 per additional child. We add $3 flat(not hourly) if there is homework help, cooking/cleaning services, or pet care needed. If sending payment on paypal, please send payment to the sitter as "friends and family".
